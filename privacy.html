This Privacy Policy explains how bitbyte ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website or services. By using our website, you agree to the terms described here.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect information you directly provide when you:

  • Fill out our contact or inquiry form (name, email, phone, project details)
  • Place an order or request a service
  • Apply for an internship (name, email, portfolio links, academic details)
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or blog updates
  • Communicate with us via email or WhatsApp

We may also automatically collect your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time on site when you browse our website.

⚙️ How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver the services you've requested (resume, portfolio, website, etc.)
  • Communicate with you about your project, updates, or revisions
  • Process payments and send receipts or invoices
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Review internship applications and contact shortlisted candidates
  • Send occasional marketing emails (only if you've opted in)
  • Improve our website and service quality

🔐 Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Full stop.

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Payment processors — to complete your transaction securely (e.g., Razorpay, UPI)
  • Legal obligations — if required by law or government authority
  • Service delivery — with team members who assist your project, under confidentiality agreements

Your project files, personal details, and communication with bitbyte are kept strictly confidential and are never shared with other clients or third parties for marketing purposes.

🍪 Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience.

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function properly
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand page visits and user behaviour (e.g., Google Analytics)

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the website.

🗄️ Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access.

  • Project files are stored only for the duration of your project plus a 30-day post-delivery window
  • Payment information is handled exclusively by our payment partners — never stored on our servers
  • Contact form data is accessed only by authorised team members

✅ Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at bitbyte.tech01@gmail.com. We'll respond within 7 business days.

👦 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

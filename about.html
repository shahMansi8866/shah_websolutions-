Our Story

🎮 HOW IT ALL STARTED

Bitbyte was born out of frustration. Watching talented people get overlooked because their resumes and portfolios didn't reflect their actual brilliance — that was unacceptable.

Founder Mansi Shah started bitbyte with a single client and a mission: make every professional look as strong on paper (and on screen) as they are in real life.

Today, bitbyte helps students, freelancers, and small businesses craft digital assets that truly represent who they are — and get results.
